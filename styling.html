HOW TO STYLE
The Beaded Braid

To create this beautiful look you will only need our black pearl bobby pins. It's so simple it will only take you about seven minutes or even less! First you start by doing a french style braid from the top of your head with three different seperate strands of hair. Then you braid all the way down, and if you are not into the neat kind of look, you can pull up on your braid to make it look a bit more natural and messier. Give it some volume.
 
The Messy but Classy Bun

This hairstyle is perfect for every occasion. You can go to a beach wedding with it, or to school. This simple but put together look will give your outfit a dainty feminine touch without looking like you tried to much. You can achieve this look with out Stella Diamond Bobbies.
