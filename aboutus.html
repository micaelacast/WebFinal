HOME SHOP STYLING ABOUT US
Based in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. We are a company that makes handmade hairclips in order to give women their desired hair style with a tropical touch with personality. We make sure to use recycled pins and natural rocks. Half of the profits made are donated to Niños por el Futuro Foundation in Barahona, hoping to make our future brighter through our youth. We make sure to make every hairclip and hair pin with love and hope to spread our country's essence.
